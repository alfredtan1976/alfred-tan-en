About Us

Master Alfred Tan Numerology Studio is an English-language Chinese metaphysics website offering free, no-registration lifestyle reference readings. The site presents traditional BaZi, Qi Men Dun Jia, Feng Shui, and numerology concepts in calm, practical language for everyday self-reflection.

Who We Are

Master Alfred Tan, also known as Chen Xuan Qing, has spent over thirty years studying traditional Chinese metaphysics, including BaZi, Qi Men Dun Jia, and spatial Feng Shui. This website turns those symbolic systems into simple digital reference tools.

What This Site Provides

Visitors can use modules for a daily fortune guide, wealth and career reflection, relationship reference, annual luck guidance, and one-question oracle-style reading. The public daily guide can be read without entering personal details.

Our Editorial Approach

The content is written to be understandable, respectful, and non-alarmist. It does not claim certainty or replace professional advice. Important decisions should always be made with real-world information and qualified guidance.
