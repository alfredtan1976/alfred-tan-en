Privacy Policy

At en.alfred-tan.info, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use this website.

1. Reading Data and LocalStorage

When you use the fortune reading tool, the name, gender, birth date, birth hour, location, and selected module you enter are used by front-end logic to generate a simulated reading. This profile is stored in your own browser LocalStorage so the page can remember you when you return.

This static page does not require account registration and does not persistently store your birth profile on our server.

2. Cookies and Google AdSense

This site may use Google AdSense as a third-party advertising provider. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's previous visits to this site or other websites. You can manage personalized ad preferences through Google Ads settings and your browser settings.

3. Contact

For privacy enquiries, contact privacy@alfred-tan.info.
